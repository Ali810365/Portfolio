The Journey |

My love for computers travels as far as I can remember. When most people make that statement, their love references the excitement that runs through us when we use these machines. Of course I felt the same way but my adoration went furthure then that. I was astonished by how such a thing works and the magical secret. Eventually I came across programming in the 7th and thats when I knew I wanted to be a programmer when I grow up. I studied many programming languages such as C++, C#, PHP, and Phyton. However at the time I didn't consider it as a career because for me it was a passion and a hobby. It wasn't until I got to college when I decided I wanted to be a web developer and that's when I learned HTML and CSS along with Javascript. Since then I have dedicted much of my time to being proficient at HTML, CSS and Javascript.
